trivia question:
Describe following MetroE services - meaning, implementation, port or vlan-based:
- EPL
    ethernet private line
        L2 point-to-point, no multiplexing, TDM, port
- EWS
    ethernet wire service
        l2 point-to-point, ces, port
- ERS
    ethernet relay service
        l3 point-to-point, vlan
- EMS
    ethernet multipoint service
        l2 multipoint service, port
- ERMS
    ethernet relay multipoint service
        l3 multipoint service, vlan
